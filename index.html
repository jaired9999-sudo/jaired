The Philosophy

BUILT TO Endure

At VÖRN, we reject disposable fashion. Every piece is conceived in our London studio and executed by master artisans across Italy, Japan, and Scotland.

We source only heritage fabrics — Super 180s wool from Zegna mills, 18.5 micron merino from Biella, and selvedge denim from Kojima, Japan.
