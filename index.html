Our Story

A pursuit of
perfection on
every plate

Aurum was born from a singular obsession — that a meal should move you the way great art does. Founded by Chef Aryan Mehta, we blend the forgotten flavours of subcontinental heritage with the precision of contemporary French technique.

Every ingredient is sourced with intention. Every plate is a composition. Every evening, an experience that begins the moment you walk through our doors.
